Te En Dictionary FAQ

  • How to lookup a meaning for telugu word?
    Telugu word must be typed in unicode format. To type a telugu word, select language option from Menu item to Telugu or by pressing F2 and type in. When finished press enter. However, some third party tools can also be used for typing.
    Third party and in house tools for typing telugu:
    • Microsoft IME
    • Barah IME
    • Lekhini
    • Google Indic Translator
    • Dileep's Telugu Converter
